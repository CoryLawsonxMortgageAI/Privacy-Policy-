‌Privacy Policy for xMortgageAI

‌Introduction


Welcome to xMortgageAI, a service developed by Cory Lawson. xMortgageAI is an advanced hybrid mortgage advisor designed to streamline the mortgage application process and assist mortgage loan officers with various tasks, from initial client communication to pre-approval. This privacy policy outlines how xMortgageAI collects, uses, and protects your personal information.


‌Information We Collect

xMortgageAI collects the following types of information to provide and improve our services:


  1. Personal Information: This includes your name, contact information, Social Security number, employment details, income information, and other details required for mortgage applications.

  2. Financial Information: xMortgageAI may collect data related to your financial status, including bank account details, credit history, income sources, assets, liabilities, and tax information, necessary for mortgage eligibility analysis and simulations.

  3. Loan Application Data: Information provided during the mortgage application process, including completed 1003 forms, credit reviews, and income and asset verification data.

  4. Document Information: Any documents uploaded or shared via the platform, including but not limited to tax returns, pay stubs, bank statements, and other financial records, which may be analyzed using Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology.

  5. Automated Data: Information automatically generated through your use of the xMortgageAI service, such as interaction logs, session data, and simulation outcomes.


‌How We Use Your Information

xMortgageAI uses your information to:


  1. Streamline the Mortgage Application Process: Automate and enhance the efficiency of tasks related to mortgage applications, including initial client communication, loan eligibility analysis, and document collection.

  2. Simulation and Analysis: Provide feedback on loan eligibility, approval likelihood, and suggestions for improving the chances of mortgage approval based on the data provided.

  3. Income and Credit Assessment: Perform complex income calculations, credit score analysis, and debt-to-income ratio calculations to assist in determining loan approval probabilities.

  4. Compliance with Loan Guidelines: Ensure all processes adhere to the latest mortgage guidelines by integrating with ALLREGS and other industry standards.

  5. CRM and LOS Integration: Facilitate seamless communication and process management by integrating with CRM and Loan Origination Systems (LOS).

  6. Security and Compliance: Protect your data and ensure compliance with relevant regulations, including safeguarding your personal and financial information.


‌How We Protect Your Information

xMortgageAI employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and financial information. These measures include:


  1. Data Encryption: All data transmitted and stored by xMortgageAI is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

  2. Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your data, and access is limited to the minimum necessary to perform their job functions.

  3. Compliance with Regulations: xMortgageAI complies with all relevant privacy and data protection regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws.

  4. Regular Audits: xMortgageAI undergoes regular security audits to ensure that our security practices are up-to-date and effective.


‌Sharing Your Information

xMortgageAI does not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to:


  1. Fulfill Your Requests: Share data with lenders, underwriters, or other financial institutions involved in your mortgage application process.

  2. Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests.

  3. Service Providers: Partner with trusted service providers to perform functions on our behalf, such as data analysis, legal compliance, and document processing. These providers are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.


‌Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:


  1. Access Your Information: Request a copy of the personal data xMortgageAI holds about you.

  2. Correct Your Information: Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal data.

  3. Delete Your Information: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

  4. Limit Processing: Request limitations on how your data is used, particularly for marketing purposes.


‌Changes to This Privacy Policy

xMortgageAI may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and, where appropriate, notifying you via email.
